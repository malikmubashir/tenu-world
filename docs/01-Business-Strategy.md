## Executive Summary

Tenu is a web application serving international tenants — students, workers, and expats — who rent in France or the UK without fully understanding their rights or the local language. The product guides them through move-in and move-out inspections in their native language, generates AI-powered risk assessments, and produces legally-framed dispute letters in French or English when needed.

**The core insight:** 445,000 international students in France (record 2024-25) and 700,000+ in the UK sign leases in languages they barely understand. Most silently accept unfair deposit deductions because they do not know they can challenge them, or how. No product currently serves them.

---

## Problem

International tenants face a compounded problem:
- They sign leases in a language they barely understand
- They do not know what their rights or obligations are from day one
- They do not know what to photograph, what to check, or what to request
- At move-out, they face an inspection in a foreign language with an unfamiliar legal framework
- They are least likely to challenge deductions — cultural reluctance + language barrier
- They are most likely to accept losses and move on

**In the UK:** 23% of tenants who enter deposit disputes submit no evidence at all — and lose by default (DPS data).  
**In France:** État des lieux is mandatory by law, yet most international tenants do not know what it means or how to use it.

---

## Solution

Tenu delivers a full tenant lifecycle product in the user's language:

1. **Move-in onboarding** — rights explained on day 1 in user's language
2. **Guided photo evidence builder** — room-by-room, timestamped, camera-only
3. **Rights explainer** — FR or UK law explained in user's language
4. **AI risk scan at move-out** — deduction risk scored by room
5. **Dispute letter generation** — FR/EN legal letter + user-language explanation side by side
6. **Community** — language-grouped peer support

## Competitive Landscape

| Player | Geography | Gap vs Tenu |
|---|---|---|
| Deposit Guard (depositguard.com) | UK only | No multilingual, no AI, no FR |
| DefendMyRent (defendmyrent.com) | US only | No inspection/evidence, no EU law |
| Compens.ai | Global broad | Not deposit-specific, no photo evidence |
| TDS / DPS / mydeposits | UK (gov) | Landlord-facing, no tenant prep tools |
| BailFacile / Matera | France | Landlord-only |

**White space confirmed:** No product combines evidence collection + AI risk scoring + jurisdiction-specific dispute letters + multilingual for international tenants in FR or UK.
