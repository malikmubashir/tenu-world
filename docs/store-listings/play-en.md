## Full description (4000 characters max)

```
Tenu helps you document your rental property and defend your security deposit, one step at a time.

Built for international students and expat tenants who are not familiar with the detail of French or British rental law, Tenu translates the applicable rules into practical actions.

What you will find inside Tenu
• A plain-language guide to tenant rights, organised by jurisdiction (France at launch; United Kingdom to follow).
• A guided inventory that does not forget anything: meter readings, locks, seals, skirting boards, paint condition.
• A risk analysis on your security deposit derived from your photos, with a displayed confidence level for each observation.
• A move-out report, time-stamped and dated, to keep as documentary evidence.
• A dispute letter module to reclaim a wrongly withheld deposit, printable or sendable as recorded delivery.

Typical journey
1. Move-in inventory — the app walks you room by room. Photograph what matters; annotate what is missing or damaged.
2. Risk analysis — Tenu reviews your photos and flags items likely to be contested, with references to the relevant legal provisions.
3. Move-out and dispute — if your landlord withholds your deposit without a clear basis, Tenu drafts a letter that fits the applicable legal framework (France: Code civil and loi du 6 juillet 1989; United Kingdom: TDS, DPS or MyDeposits alternative dispute resolution).

What Tenu is not
Tenu is not a law firm and does not replace legal advice. The app provides tools and documentary support; the decision to escalate remains yours. Legal content is reviewed by qualified professionals in each jurisdiction before publication.

Payment and privacy
The move-in diagnostic is priced between 15 € and 35 € inclusive of VAT depending on the size of the property. The dispute letter module is priced separately. All payments are processed by Stripe; Tenu never stores payment card data. Your photos are hosted on European servers (Cloudflare R2 — Frankfurt region) and can be deleted on request from inside the app.

Languages
French and English at launch. Arabic, Chinese, Hindi and Urdu are in preparation.

Publisher
Published by Global Apex.Net SAS, registered in France (Versailles Commercial Registry 941 666 067). Support: support@tenu.world. Data protection: dpo@tenu.world.
```

## Data safety section (Play form)

Data collected and linked to user identity:
- Email address — authentication and report delivery.
- Photos — subject of the service: analysis of the rented property.
- User identifiers — technical link with the Supabase database.
- Product interaction — telemetry on our own funnel.

Data shared with third parties:
- Stripe (payments): IP address, card information, billing email. DPA signed.
- Brevo (email): email address, transactional content. DPA signed.
- Cloudflare R2 (photo storage): image files. DPA signed. Frankfurt region (EU).
- Anthropic (AI analysis): photo contents (read-only, not retained by Anthropic). DPA signed.

Account deletion: available from the app. Deletes photos, scans, letters, email.
Data encrypted in transit: yes (HTTPS/TLS throughout).
Data security: yes, GDPR compliance and standard contractual clauses.
