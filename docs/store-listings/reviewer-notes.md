## What the app does

Tenu is a tenant rights companion for international students and expats in France (UK support disabled at launch). Users:

1. Create a move-in inspection (état des lieux) with room-by-room photos
2. Receive an AI-generated risk score per room (Anthropic Claude Haiku, server-side only)
3. Optionally generate a dispute letter (Claude Sonnet) for contested deposit deductions
4. Pay per inspection at upload (Stripe, TVA-inclusive pricing)

No subscription. No account creation required beyond a magic-link email.

## Permissions used and why

| Permission | Platform | Justification |
|---|---|---|
| Camera | iOS + Android | Required to photograph rooms during move-in / move-out inspection |
| Photo library (read) | iOS | User may upload existing images instead of taking new ones |
| Photo library (add) | iOS | Declared in Info.plist but never exercised — required by Apple policy when the read permission is declared |
| Internet | Android | API calls to tenu.world for AI processing and Stripe payment |
| Network state | Android | Offline detection for upload queue drain |

No location, contacts, microphone, or notification permissions are requested.

## Payment flow (Reader App model)

Payment is completed on the web (tenu.world) before the native app is used. The native app does not contain any in-app purchase flow and does not trigger StoreKit or Google Play Billing. This follows the Reader App exemption. Reviewers can test all app functionality with the demo account above without entering payment details.

---

## AI-generated content disclosure

- Risk scores are generated by Anthropic Claude Haiku (server-side, tenu.world API)
- Dispute letters are generated by Anthropic Claude Sonnet (server-side)
- AI calls are made exclusively from Next.js route handlers — no Anthropic API key is present in the app bundle
- Legal rights explainers (10 facts per jurisdiction) are static, pre-written content, not AI-generated

---

## Data handling

- Photos are stored encrypted at rest in Cloudflare R2 (EU region)
- Supabase Postgres (EU West) for user data and inspection metadata
- No data is shared with third parties beyond sub-processors listed in the Privacy Policy

## Localization

UI languages at launch: French (FR), English (EN). The app detects locale from the device and falls back to French. Legal output (risk scores, dispute letters) is always in FR or EN regardless of UI locale.

---

## Known limitations at v1.0.0

- UK jurisdiction is disabled. The "UK" option does not appear in the jurisdiction picker.
- Colocation (shared flat with multiple tenants) is supported as a single-inspection flow; separate per-tenant reports are a post-launch feature.
