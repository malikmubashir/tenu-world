## What is Tenu?

Tenu is a universal tenant rights companion for international residents — guiding them through move-in to move-out in their own language, with AI-powered risk scoring and jurisdiction-specific dispute support for France and the UK.

**Tagline:** Your rights. Your language. Your deposit.
